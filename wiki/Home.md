## Product Summary

Student Productivity Hub is a mobile-first study operating system with:

- OAuth login through multiple identity providers.
- Role-based feature access for sensitive modules.
- Assignment and planner management.
- Pomodoro timer with study/break logs.
- GPA calculation and progress analytics.
- Quiz authoring, adapter import/export, adaptive runtime, and review scheduling.
- Data portability via export/import and local backup restore.
